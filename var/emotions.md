##  Anger

- **Somatic Signature:** A violent, instantaneous sympathetic redlining of the entire system. Breathing becomes deep, rapid, and sharp, or locks into a pressurized, heavy hold in the upper chest. Heart rate surges dramatically, creating a bounding, rhythmic pounding in the chest, carotid arteries, and ears as blood pressure spikes. Vasodilation shifts massive volume to the major skeletal muscle groups and the face, causing a burning internal heat. Muscular tone is intensely braced and coiled for kinetic impact—the jaw locks, the brow knits down, the fists clench, and the spine tethers forward, driving a direct, unblinking visual lock on the target.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The system detects an immediate, illegitimate barrier, threat, or boundary violation along its vector of intent. It occurs when an external agent or obstacle restricts the system's agency, compromises its territory, or violates a deeply held rule of engagement, provoking a sudden calculation that the resistance must be violently neutralized to preserve structural integrity.
    
- **Action Tendency:** High-velocity forward locomotion, direct kinetic confrontation, loud and low-frequency vocalization, and actions designed to physically dismantle, breach, or terrify the obstacle into submission.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Serves as the ultimate boundary-enforcement and survival engine. By mobilizing maximum raw kinetic force and suppressing pain or fear responses in a fraction of a second, it allows the organism to violently repel predators, break through paralyzing constraints, and defend its vital resources or loved ones against predatory exploitation.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Blind, recursive destruction and structural self-sabotage. When executed without strict tactical control, the surge obliterates executive function and long-term predictive mapping. The system attacks indiscriminately, burning massive metabolic reserves, destroying critical alliance networks, and causing catastrophic, irreversible damage to its own perimeter over a passing or imagined friction.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** The complete neutralization, destruction, or definitive submission of the opposing obstacle; a sudden, overwhelming counter-threat that forces an emergency shift into survival panic; or the gradual metabolic exhaustion of the sympathetic surge as adrenaline reserves drain from the tissue.

##  Anticipation and Suspense

- **Somatic Signature:** High-velocity, forward-leaning up-regulation of the nervous system. Breathing is shallow, held at the top of the inhalation, or executed in rapid, quiet cycles to minimize internal acoustic noise. Heart rate is elevated and hyper-reactive, producing a distinct, pounding sensation in the chest and throat. Muscular tone is intensely coiled and primed for explosive action; the entire skeletal structure is slightly flexed, eyes are wide and locked on the primary vector of arrival, and the skin experiences a prickling, hyper-sensitized electrical charge.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The system isolates a highly significant, imminent event or outcome that is locked onto the immediate timeline but has not yet broken across the threshold of the present moment. It occurs when the predictive engine has fully mapped the _arrival trajectory_ of a massive variable, but the _valence_ (catastrophic threat or massive resource windfall) remains unresolved, suspended in a state of high probability.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Total perceptual lock on the incoming vector, complete inhibition of non-essential metabolic functions (such as digestion or sleep induction), and an intense kinetic readiness to spring into immediate flight, defense, or acquisition the millisecond the threshold is crossed.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Minimizes systemic latency to absolute zero. By pre-loading the muscular and metabolic engines with raw fuel before the event occurs, it ensures the organism can react with instantaneous, maximum velocity the moment the variable manifests, gaining a critical fraction-of-a-second advantage in high-stakes environments.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Chronically burns out the structural engine. If the state is maintained for extended intervals without resolution, the constant sympathetic redlining drains metabolic reserves, damages cellular tissue via prolonged cortisol and adrenaline exposure, fractures cognitive cohesion, and leaves the system jittery, exhausted, and brittle.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** The definite arrival and manifestation of the anticipated event—instantly converting the suspended potential into immediate defensive action, consumption, or baseline relief—or a structural collapse of the timeline that reliably removes the event from the horizon.

##  Awe

- **Somatic Signature:** A profound, breathless suspension localized in the chest and throat, accompanied by a distinct sensation of cool expansion throughout the upper torso. Breathing halts momentarily at the peak of a deep, silent inhalation, followed by an exceptionally slow, relaxed exhalation. Heart rate slows to a calm, resonant baseline with high parasympathetic tone. The jaw drops slightly, the eyes widen completely to maximize the visual field, and a wave of prickling, electrical chills (piloerection) ripples across the skin, particularly on the arms and back of the neck. The body feels small, lightweight, and completely anchored to the ground.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The system encounters an entity, space, or concept of such vast magnitude, complexity, or structural scale that it instantly transcends the current capacity of the internal predictive model. It occurs when the observer is forced to accommodate a reality that is grander than its existing cognitive architecture, prompting an immediate, voluntary downsizing of the self’s perceived scale relative to the cosmos.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Absolute kinetic stillness, sustained and un-blaced perceptual focus, and a total cessation of self-referential or transactional behaviors. The system is propelled to simply remain present, absorb data, and adjust its internal maps to match the vastness of the object.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Forces a radical, necessary expansion of the internal processing architecture. By shattering small, rigid models of reality and suppressing petty, self-interested drives, it opens the system to massive cognitive upgrades, fosters profound collective alignment with the wider environment, and restores a balanced perspective on personal significance.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Passive fixation or existential overwhelm. If the system is unable to process or ground the vastness of the data, the state can dissolve into a paralyzing, submissive quietism. The individual may become completely detached from immediate tactical survival, floating in a state of un-anchored wonder while ignoring urgent, real-world maintenance requirements.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** The gradual integration of the vast data into a updated, expanded internal map, a shift in attention back to local, immediate tasks, or the entry of a sharp threat vector into the perimeter that demands an instant return to defensive, self-preserves execution.

##  Boredom

- **Somatic Signature:** A heavy, draining lethargy paired with an undercurrent of restless, nervous agitation. Breathing is exceptionally slow, shallow, and punctuated by frequent, deep sighs that deflate the torso. Heart rate drops to a flat, sluggish baseline but remains hyper-reactive to brief distractions. Muscular tone is characterized by an un-anchored, slouching collapse—the spine slumps, the head rests heavily on the hands, and the limbs feel leaden, yet the fingers tap or the legs twitch rhythmically. The eyes glaze over, losing focus and drifting aimlessly across the room.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The system registers that the current environment or task contains zero high-utility data streams, novel variables, or actionable vectors for resource acquisition. It occurs when the processing engine is fully pre-loaded with energy but finds nothing in the local field that justifies the metabolic cost of execution, signaling that the current space is a dead zone for systemic growth.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Errant scanning, frequent shifts in body position, random task-switching, and an urgent propulsion to alter the immediate sensory field. The system is driven to abandon its current location or disrupt the status quo simply to provoke a data return.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Acts as a vital migratory and evolutionary engine. By making prolonged stasis and low-utility environments intensely uncomfortable, it forces the organism to break out of comfort zones, seek out new territories, develop new skills, or innovate raw strategies during periods of absolute safety when survival is not actively threatened.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Drifts into reckless, self-destructive stimulation-seeking. If the system cannot migrate or find constructive output, the intolerable friction can compel it to generate artificial crises—sabotaging stable relationships, engaging in high-risk behaviors, or consuming toxic inputs—just to escape the agony of processing a vacuum.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** The sudden introduction of a high-stakes variable or threat into the perimeter, the deliberate choice to engage in a highly complex, deeply challenging skill track, or a physical migration to a completely novel territory that demands active mapping.

##  Confusion

- **Somatic Signature:** A distinct, localized tension concentrated in the brow, eyes, and forehead, accompanied by an unsettling, un-centered flutter in the upper stomach. Breathing becomes irregular, shifting between brief, shallow holds and deep, frustrated exhalations. Heart rate exhibits a jagged, mildly elevated cadence. Muscular tone is characterized by an asymmetric bracing—the head tilts slightly, the eyes narrow or dart between points, and the hands may rise in tentative, open gestures. The global sensation is one of internal friction and cognitive heat.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The system encounters low-level, conflicting data streams or a minor, persistent breakdown in its localized predictive model. Unlike shock (which completely demolishes the map) or disorientation (which erases coordinates), confusion occurs when the map is present but contains mutually exclusive tracks or ambiguous variables ($A$ and $B$ are both true, but they cannot coexist).
    
- **Action Tendency:** A localized deceleration of all physical output, paired with a persistent, iterative scanning of the immediate problem. The system loops its attention back over the anomalous data points, testing connections and attempting to force the pieces into a logical architecture.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Serves as a vital error-correcting pause. By jamming the gears of forward execution, it prevents the system from acting on corrupted, incomplete, or flawed data. It forces the cognitive engine to slow down, isolate the specific logic gap, and verify the terrain before burning more energy on a false premise.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Drifts into a recursive, exhausting processing loop. If the system lacks the necessary variables to resolve the paradox, it can become chronically stuck in a spinning-wheel state. This burns massive cognitive and metabolic reserves on an un-solvable knot, causing mental fatigue, frustration, and eventual executive burnout.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** Isolating the missing variable that bridges the contradiction, choosing a single track to test through immediate action (forcing a data return), or voluntarily abandoning the specific problem to restore baseline processing efficiency.

##  Contempt

- **Somatic Signature:** A sharp, asymmetrical tightening localized almost entirely on one side of the face and upper torso. Breathing is slight, controlled, and executed through a single, quiet nostril-flare or a brief, dismissive snort. Heart rate remains flat, cool, and unaffected by the target's presence. Muscular tone features a rigid, unilateral lift—the sneer pulls one corner of the lip upward, the chin tilts backward to force a downward gaze, and the shoulders rotate slightly away from the target, creating a physical stance of structural distancing.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The internal observer executes a definitive, top-down evaluation of an external agent, concluding that the target is fundamentally low-value, incompetent, or structurally useless to the collective hierarchy. It occurs when the predictive engine determines that the target has violated basic standards of functional capability or ethical integrity, prompting a calculation that the target does not warrant the metabolic energy of anger or confrontation, but rather immediate exclusion.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Unilateral dismissal, cold mockery, eye-rolling, and physical withdrawal. The system is propelled to cut off all transactional loops with the target, treat their input as noise, and actively signal their diminished status to the wider network.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Serves as a vital hygiene and boundary-maintenance engine for the collective hierarchy. By cleanly deprioritizing low-value, parasitic, or corrupting elements without burning the metabolic fuel required for active warfare, it preserves the system's structural integrity, reinforces shared competence standards, and insulates the group from internal rot.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Breeds blind arrogance and catastrophic tribal fracturing. If the evaluation is driven by unverified data or raw hubris, the system prematurely dismisses genuine external threats or ignores highly valuable corrective feedback from below, creating a brittle, isolated architecture that is entirely vulnerable to an unexpected counter-strike.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** The target executing a verifiable, undeniable demonstration of competence or structural utility that forces a re-indexing of their status; a complete physical or operational separation that removes the target from the system's territory entirely; or a collapse of the observer’s own status that renders their judgment obsolete.

##  Curiosity

- **Somatic Signature:** A forward-leaning, highly focused up-regulation of the internal system. Breathing is quiet, steady, and light, minimizing internal noise to maximize external data collection. Heart rate is slightly elevated but remains fluid and stable, supplying an alert, bright energy to the brain. Muscular tone is characterized by an open, un-braced readiness—the head tilts, the torso leans slightly toward the object of focus, and the eyes widen, darting deliberately to track details. The internal sensation is a pleasant, driving warmth.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The system detects a localized gap in its predictive model, paired with a concurrent calculation that the missing information is both accessible and highly valuable. It occurs when an object, pattern, or environment presents a optimal ratio of the familiar to the unfamiliar, signaling a low-risk, high-reward opportunity to expand the system's map.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Active exploration, physical approach, manipulation of the object, and persistent tracking. The system is propelled to look behind, peer inside, dissect, or ask questions to extract the hidden data structure.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Expands the territory of the known and upgrades systemic fitness. By driving the organism to proactively seek out and master new variables during periods of relative safety, it builds an exceptionally robust, rich predictive map, ensuring the self is prepared for future environmental shifts before a crisis forces adaptation.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Reckless exposure to hidden hazards or major resource waste. If executed without proper perimeter tracking, the drive can compel the system to breach a safety boundary, stumble into a predatory nest, or burn massive energy stores investigating low-utility anomalies that yield zero return on investment.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** Exhausting the novel data stream and fully integrating the structural mechanics of the object into the internal map, or the sudden emergence of a higher-priority threat or resource vector that demands an immediate shift in focus.

##  Disorientation

- **Somatic Signature:** A sudden, floating suspension of bodily anchor points. Breathing becomes shallow, rapid, and un-centered; heart rate exhibits an irregular, fluttering rhythm. Muscular tone is characterized by a fluid, un-braced instability—knees feel soft, and the baseline vestibular orientation (balance) feels slightly uncoupled from the horizon. Eye movement becomes rapid and saccadic, shifting focus across the field of view without locking onto any single target.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The system experiences a massive, abrupt mismatch between its internal predictive model of space, time, or narrative context and the immediate environmental data. It occurs when the coordinate system the observer uses to track reality is scrambled, leaving the system temporarily unable to locate its current position on the map.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Instantaneous behavioral freezing, physical reaching or leaning toward solid structural anchors, and a frantic, multi-sensory scanning of the immediate surroundings to find a recognizable baseline.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Halts all forward kinetic output when navigating blind. By forcing an immediate stop, it prevents the organism from walking off a ledge, burning energy in the wrong direction, or executing catastrophic tactical errors based on an inverted map, preserving options until the data can be re-indexed.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Prolonged vertigo and systemic panic. If the system fails to re-establish a reliable baseline, the freezing response mutates into acute anxiety, fracturing executive function, causing motor clumsiness, and leaving the individual entirely vulnerable to ambient hazards due to sheer processing paralysis.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** Locating a single, immutable structural landmark (physical, logical, or temporal) that allows the internal observer to re-calibrate its coordinates and rebuild a coherent map of the current territory.

##  Dread

- **Somatic Signature:** Chronic, low-grade sympathetic tone paired with persistent muscular bracing. Sensation of a heavy, cold anchor or constriction in the upper abdomen and chest. Breathing is shallow and confined to the thoracic cavity; resting heart rate remains elevated without kinetic release. Persistent muscle tension locks the jaw, neck, and shoulders. Metabolic resources are steadily diverted away from digestion and repair, causing gastric sluggishness.
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The system projects a high-probability, unavoidable future drop in safety, health, or resource baselines. Unlike fear, the trigger is non-localized and detached from the immediate sensory environment; it is an un-falsifiable countdown to an inevitable negative outcome on the timeline.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Hyper-vigilant scanning of the conceptual future, strict conservation of immediate physical energy reserves, and aggressive avoidance of any novel risks that might accelerate the timeline of depletion.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Serves as a defensive survival buffer. By treating the upcoming siege as a certainty, it forces the system to hoard resources, reduce exposure, and mentally simulate the worst-case scenario so it isn't caught flat-footed when the blow lands.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ The system burns massive metabolic reserves on a passive stakeout against a ghost. Because the threat is structural and prospective, the body remains in a toxic, non-kinetic holding pattern that erodes internal organs, causes sleep fragmentation, and paralyzes present-moment agency.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** The feared event finally occurs (shifting the system out of anticipation and into active crisis management), or the future timeline is definitively rewritten by a verifiable, structural alteration in the environment.

- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Serves as an immediate social repair mechanism. By broadcasting a visible, involuntary signal of distress and alignment with the group's standards, it disarms potential judgment or hostility from the audience, demonstrating that the individual recognizes the mistake and respects the shared social boundary.

- **Dissolution Criteria:** An explicit, reassuring gesture from the audience (such as shared laughter or validation) that closes the rupture, a rapid, successful re-curation of the public mask, or physical removal from the presence of the witnesses.

This burns valuable social credit, destroys cooperative networks, and leaves the individual bitter, stagnant, and blind to their own development.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** Acquiring an equivalent or superior resource/status to restore systemic balance, witnessing the target lose the contested advantage (collapsing the comparison), or structurally shifting the self into an entirely different hierarchy where the target is no longer a relevant peer.

##  Exhaustion and Burnout

Breathing is shallow and flat; resting heart rate may remain abnormally elevated or stubbornly resistant to exertion. Heavy, leaden muscular tone dominates the limbs, paired with chronic neural fatigue, cognitive fog, and a blunted sensory apparatus. The internal system has run its operational budgets into a severe deficit, triggering an involuntary systemic circuit breaker to halt further resource depletion.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Total behavioral flatline and withdrawal. The system drops its responsiveness to both threats and rewards, ignoring external cues that would normally trigger panic or desire, and seeks absolute immobility and isolation.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Acts as an emergency preservation mechanism. By rendering the system numb to external demands, it forces a cessation of the catastrophic spending loop, saving the organism from permanent structural, neural, or organ damage through non-negotiable stasis.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ When the system drops its defenses completely while in a high-risk environment, it leaves itself dangerously exposed. The flatline state can freeze necessary survival behaviors, causing the individual to abandon vital social roles, ignore critical maintenance requirements, and sink into a long-term, non-adaptive vegetative low-power mode.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** An extended period of zero-demand rest paired with biochemical restoration, or a severe, unmistakable existential threat that overrides the circuit breaker via an adrenaline surge to force a final, desperate survival effort.

##  Fear

Blood is instantly shunted from the viscera and skin to the large skeletal muscles, causing a cold sweat, sudden paleness, and a dropping sensation in the stomach. Breathing freezes or becomes rapid and shallow; heart rate skyrockets. Muscular tone is intensely coiled, primed for explosive kinetic output or total immobility (freezing).
    
- **Generative Mechanics:** The immediate detection of a localized, unambiguous, and present threat to the system's physical integrity or immediate survival. The danger is right here, right now, requiring no cognitive projection to decode.
    
- **Action Tendency:** Instantaneous evasion, flight, or explosive self-defense. If movement is calculated as impossible or counterproductive, the system defaults to absolute immobility to avoid detection.
    
- **Action Teleology:**
    
    - _Optimal Execution:_ Minimizes reaction time to zero through subconscious, reflexive overriding of the deliberate mind. It maximizes the immediate structural survival of the organism by using raw kinetic force to escape or neutralize a lethal threat.
        
    - _Sub-Optimal Execution:_ Panic causes catastrophic motor errors, blind rushing into greater danger, or a total freezing response when movement is the only viable escape route. It overrides rational environmental assessment, treating benign variables as lethal threats.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** The complete removal, destruction, or outrunning of the threat, allowing the sympathetic surge to break and clear through shivering or trembling as the system resets.

##  Guilt

- **Action Tendency:** A powerful, restless drive toward kinetic repair, confession, restitution, and the direct undoing of the damage caused. The system is propelled toward the object of the violation to balance the internal ledger.

If the damage is truly irreversible, or if the system cannot find a path to restitution, the energy becomes trapped in a recursive loop. The individual burns massive metabolic and cognitive reserves punishing themselves over a static point on the timeline, paralyzing present-moment utility without altering the past.
        
- **Dissolution Criteria:** Executing a verifiable act of restitution or repair that balances the ledger, receiving explicit forgiveness or absolution from the damaged entity, or completing a structured, internal penance that satisfies the observer's ethical code.

##  Humiliation
